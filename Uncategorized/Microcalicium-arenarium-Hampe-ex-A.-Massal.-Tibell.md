## Introduction
Microcalicium is a genus  of lichen-forming fungi in the order Pertusariales. It is the only genus in the monotypic family Microcaliciaceae. These taxa were circumscribed by the Finnish lichenologist Edvard August Vainio in 1927, with Microcalicium disseminatum assigned as the type species.

## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in bright, indirect light to partial sun, typical of its native temperate sandy environments. Watering should be infrequent, allowing the substrate to dry completely, and no supplemental fertilization is needed. Emulate well-drained, open conditions for optimal growth.

### Fertilizer
No fertilizer is required. Lichens are self-sufficient, deriving nutrients from the air, rain, and the substrate itself.
