The Pygmy Buttercup thrives in cool, moist conditions characteristic of alpine and arctic tundra. Provide consistently moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sun, especially avoiding intense afternoon sun in warmer climates. It requires minimal fertilizer, adapting well to nutrient-poor environments.
