Horseradish (Armoracia rusticana, syn. Cochlearia armoracia) is a perennial plant of the family Brassicaceae (which also includes mustard, wasabi, broccoli, cabbage, and radish). ## Care Guide
Horseradish is a resilient plant best grown in full sun with consistently moist, fertile soil. Regular watering and annual fertilization with organic matter or a balanced feed will ensure strong growth and high-quality root production, making it a straightforward addition to temperate gardens.
