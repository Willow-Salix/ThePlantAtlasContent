## Care Guide
This resilient perennial thrives in full sun and well-draining, lean soils, typical of its native Mediterranean coastal environment. It is highly drought-tolerant and requires minimal water once established, particularly during its summer dormancy, making it a low-maintenance choice for dry gardens.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Thrives in lean, poor to average soils. A light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring can be beneficial, but avoid over-fertilizing.
