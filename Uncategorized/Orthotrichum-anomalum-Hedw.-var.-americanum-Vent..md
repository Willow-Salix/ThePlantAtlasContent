## Care Guide
This moss thrives in a humid environment with moderate indirect light to partial shade. It requires consistent moisture, either through frequent misting or a terrarium setup, but its substrate should be allowed to dry slightly between waterings. Fertilization is rarely needed due to its low nutrient demands.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer needed. Nutrients are primarily absorbed from air and rainwater. If growth is very poor, a highly diluted balanced liquid fertilizer applied very sparingly (once a year) may be considered.
