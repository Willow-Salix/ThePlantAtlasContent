## Care Guide
Nardia lescurii thrives in cool, consistently moist, and humid environments with low, indirect light, replicating its native temperate forest floor habitat. ### Fertilizer
Very low to none. Liverworts obtain most nutrients from their environment; excessive fertilization can be harmful. If needed, use an extremely diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer very sparingly.
