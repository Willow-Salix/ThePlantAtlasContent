## Introduction
Stellaria media, chickweed, is an annual flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae. It is native to Eurasia and naturalized throughout the world, where it is a weed of waste ground, farmland and gardens. It is sometimes grown as a salad crop or for poultry consumption.

## Care Guide
Chickweed is a highly adaptable plant that thrives in cool, moist temperate conditions. Provide it with regular watering to keep the soil consistently damp and position it where it receives partial to full sun, preferably with some shade during the hottest parts of the day.
