## Care Guide
This bog sedge requires consistently wet soil and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Replicate its native temperate wetland environment by ensuring constant moisture and providing a moderately rich, organic growing medium.

### Fertilizer
Moderate to rich organic soil; benefits from regular, balanced fertilization during the growing season, especially if not planted in naturally rich soil.
