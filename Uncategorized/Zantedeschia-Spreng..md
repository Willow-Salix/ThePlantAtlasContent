## Care Guide
Calla Lilies thrive in conditions that mimic their native Southern African wetlands, requiring bright indirect to partial sun and consistently moist to wet soil. They benefit from regular feeding during their growing cycle to support lush foliage and abundant blooms.

### Fertilizer
Moderate to high; feed with a balanced liquid fertilizer every 2-4 weeks during the active growing and blooming season.
