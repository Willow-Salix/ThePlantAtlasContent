## Care Guide
Spotted Gum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, replicating its native open woodland habitat. Young plants need regular watering for establishment, but mature trees are very drought-tolerant. Fertilization should be minimal, as these plants are adapted to poorer soils.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Generally adapted to nutrient-poor soils. Use a slow-release, low-phosphorus fertilizer sparingly for young plants; established trees rarely need fertilization.
