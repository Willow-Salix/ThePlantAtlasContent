## Care Guide
This wetland willow thrives in consistently wet to saturated soil conditions, mirroring its native bog and fen habitats. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is tolerant of nutrient-poor soils. Ensure high moisture levels are maintained for optimal growth.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; adapted to nutrient-poor wetland soils, so heavy fertilization is generally not needed.
