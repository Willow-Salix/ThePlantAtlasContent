## Care Guide
This fern thrives in high humidity and indirect light, characteristic of its native rainforest understory. Maintain consistently moist, well-draining soil and provide light fertilization during active growth to mimic its natural epiphytic or lithophytic nutrient intake from decaying organic matter.

### Fertilizer
Light feeding during the active growing season (spring and summer) with a diluted liquid fertilizer. As an epiphyte/lithophyte, it is not a heavy feeder and can be sensitive to over-fertilization.
