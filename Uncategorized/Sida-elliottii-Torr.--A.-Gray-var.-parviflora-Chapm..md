## Care Guide
To care for Pineland Fanpetals, provide full sun exposure to mimic its native open pineland environment. Water moderately, allowing the soil to dry between applications, and apply fertilizer sparingly as it is adapted to less nutrient-rich soils.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate fertilization. A light application of a balanced slow-release granular fertilizer in early spring, or a diluted liquid feed every 4-6 weeks during the growing season, is sufficient. Avoid heavy feeding as it's adapted to relatively lean pineland soils.
