## Care Guide
This adaptable wild garlic thrives in temperate climates, preferring full sun to partial shade. It requires consistently moist, well-drained soil during its active growing season. Fertilization needs are low to moderate, making it suitable for average garden soils.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; thrives in average to moderately rich, well-drained soil. Light fertilization can be applied annually if soil nutrients are poor.
