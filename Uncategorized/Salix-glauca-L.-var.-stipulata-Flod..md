## Care Guide
This willow thrives in cold, open environments, requiring full to partial sun exposure. It needs consistently moist to wet soil conditions, mimicking its native boggy or meltwater-rich habitats. Fertilization should be low to moderate, as it's adapted to nutrient-poor tundra soils.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Tundra soils are typically nutrient-poor. Fertilize sparingly, perhaps once annually in spring with a balanced, slow-release formula if growth is desired or the soil is exceptionally lean.
