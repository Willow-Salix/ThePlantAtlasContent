## Introduction
Geranium sanguineum, common name bloody crane's-bill or bloody geranium, is a species of hardy flowering herbaceous perennial plant in the cranesbill family Geraniaceae. It is the county flower of Northumberland.

## Care Guide
Bloody Geranium is a low-maintenance plant that prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil. It is highly drought-tolerant once established, requiring infrequent watering and minimal to no fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. It tolerates poor to average soil fertility and generally does not require heavy fertilization.
