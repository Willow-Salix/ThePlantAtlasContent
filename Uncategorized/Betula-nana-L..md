## Care Guide
Dwarf Birch requires full sun and consistently moist, acidic soil, mirroring its native tundra and bog habitats. It thrives in cold temperatures and is not suitable for hot, dry climates. Provide minimal fertilization, as it is adapted to nutrient-poor conditions.
