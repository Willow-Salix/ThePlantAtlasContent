## Care Guide
Woolly Senna thrives in bright light conditions, from full sun to partial shade, reflecting its montane and open-habitat origins. It requires regular watering during growth, ensuring well-draining soil to prevent root rot, and benefits from moderate fertilization. Allow soil to dry slightly between waterings.

### Fertilizer
Moderate fertilization during the growing season with a balanced, all-purpose fertilizer. As a legume, it may require less nitrogen.
