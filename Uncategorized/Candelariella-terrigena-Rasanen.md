## Introduction
Candelariella is a genus of bright yellow, ocher, or greenish yellow crustose or squamulose lichens in the family Candelariaceae. Members of the genus are commonly called eggyolk lichens, goldspeck lichens, or yolk lichens.

## Care Guide
To care for Eggyolk Lichen, provide high light exposure and ensure cold, airy conditions, mimicking its native arctic-alpine habitat. Offer intermittent moisture, allowing for dry periods, and never fertilize, as lichens are sensitive to excess nutrients.

### Fertilizer
None. Lichens do not require traditional fertilization and can be harmed by nutrient-rich conditions. They derive necessary elements from the air and substrate.
