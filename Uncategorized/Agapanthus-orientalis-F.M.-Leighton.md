## Care Guide
The African Lily, hailing from South Africa's Mediterranean climate, thrives in full sun and requires moderate watering during its spring and summer growing season, becoming quite drought-tolerant once established. It benefits from a light spring fertilization to encourage its characteristic vibrant blooms, with minimal care needed during its winter dormancy.

### Fertilizer
Light feeding with a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring to support flowering. Native soils in these regions are not excessively rich, so heavy fertilization is unnecessary and can lead to more foliage than flowers.
