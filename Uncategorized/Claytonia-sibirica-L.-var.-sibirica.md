## Care Guide
Siberian springbeauty thrives in the cool, moist, and shady environment of a temperate forest understory. It requires consistently moist, organically rich, and well-draining soil, along with protection from intense afternoon sun. Provide light, organic fertilization in early spring to support its growth.

### Fertilizer
