## Care Guide
Stellaria labradorica thrives in cool, subarctic conditions with consistently moist, well-drained soil. Provide partial sun or bright indirect light, protecting it from strong direct afternoon sun. Fertilize very lightly to replicate its naturally lean native environment.

### Fertilizer
Low. Its native soils are often nutrient-poor. Fertilize sparingly with a diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer once or twice during the active growing season, or incorporate a small amount of compost.
