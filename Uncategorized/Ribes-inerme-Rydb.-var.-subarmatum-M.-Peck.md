## Care Guide
This plant thrives in the consistently moist, organically rich soils typical of its native western North American montane and riparian habitats. It prefers full sun to partial shade, requiring regular watering to ensure the soil remains moist. An annual application of compost or a balanced fertilizer in spring will support vigorous growth.
