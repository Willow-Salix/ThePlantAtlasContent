## Introduction
Rhododendron minus var. chapmanii (syn. Rhododendron chapmanii), also known as Chapman's rhododendron, is an endangered variety, endemic to Florida, of the evergreen Piedmont rhododendron.

## Care Guide
Chapman's rhododendron thrives in consistently moist, acidic soil, mimicking its native boggy habitat. Provide bright indirect light to partial sun and fertilize sparingly with an acid-forming product specifically designed for ericaceous plants. Ensure the soil never dries out completely.

### Fertilizer
Light fertilization with an acid-forming fertilizer for ericaceous plants during growing season
