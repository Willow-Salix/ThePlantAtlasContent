## Care Guide
This cold-hardy grass is best suited for full sun exposure and well-drained, infertile soils, replicating its alpine and arctic tundra environment. Water moderately, allowing the soil to dry between waterings, and use fertilizer sparingly to maintain its natural vigor and prevent overgrowth.

### Fertilizer
Low fertilizer. Native soils are typically nutrient-poor. Apply a very diluted, balanced fertilizer once a year, or none if planted in rich enough soil, to avoid over-fertilization.
