## Introduction
Calamagrostis canadensis is a species of grass, having three or more varieties, in the family Poaceae. It is known variously by the common names of bluejoint, bluejoint reedgrass, marsh reedgrass, Canadian reedgrass, meadow pinegrass, Canada bluejoint and marsh pinegrass.

## Care Guide
This wetland grass thrives in consistently wet conditions, tolerating full sun to partial shade. It requires ample water and benefits from the nutrient-rich environment typical of its native marshy habitats, making heavy fertilization generally unnecessary. Mimicking its wetland origin is key to its successful cultivation.

### Fertilizer
