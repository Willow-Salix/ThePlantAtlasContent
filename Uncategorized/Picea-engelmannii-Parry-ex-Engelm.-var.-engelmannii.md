## Introduction
Picea engelmannii, with the common names Engelmann spruce, white spruce, mountain spruce, and silver spruce, is a species of spruce native to western North America. It is highly prized for producing distinctive tone wood for acoustic guitars and other instruments, it is mostly a high-elevation mountain tree but also appears in watered canyons.

## Care Guide
Engelmann spruce requires a cool, moist environment reminiscent of its native subalpine forests. Provide full sun, consistently moist but well-drained soil, and avoid high heat or drought. Minimal fertilization is typically needed.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Native soils are typically not overly rich. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer can be applied sparingly if soil nutrients are poor.
