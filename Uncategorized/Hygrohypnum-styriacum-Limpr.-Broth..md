## Care Guide
This moss thrives in cold, perpetually wet conditions, mimicking its native alpine stream environment. Ensure constant moisture, high humidity, and provide partial shade to indirect bright light to prevent desiccation and maintain cool temperatures. Fertilizer is generally not required.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Mosses are not heavy feeders and can be harmed by high nutrient concentrations. They typically absorb sufficient nutrients from water and the surrounding environment.
