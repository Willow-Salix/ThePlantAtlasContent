## Introduction
Ramalina is a genus of greenish fruticose lichens that grow in the form of flattened, strap-like branches. Members of the genus are commonly called strap lichens or cartilage lichens. Apothecia are lecanorine.
It is in the family Ramalinaceae and in the suborder Lecanorineae.

## Care Guide
Cartilage lichen thrives in environments mimicking its native coastal habitat, requiring consistently high atmospheric humidity, frequent misting, and good air circulation. Provide bright indirect light to partial sun, and absolutely no traditional soil-based fertilizers, as it absorbs nutrients directly from the air and water.

### Fertilizer
No traditional fertilizer needed. Lichens obtain all necessary nutrients from airborne particles, dissolved minerals in rainwater, and dust. Chemical fertilizers can be harmful.
