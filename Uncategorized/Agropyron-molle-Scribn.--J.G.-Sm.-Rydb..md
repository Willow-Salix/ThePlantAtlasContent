## Care Guide
Bluebunch Wheatgrass is a highly resilient and drought-tolerant grass best suited for full sun environments. It requires minimal watering once established and generally needs little to no supplemental fertilizer, making it an excellent, low-maintenance choice for dry landscapes. Ensure well-drained soil to prevent root issues.
