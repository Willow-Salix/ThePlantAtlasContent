## Care Guide
This cup grass thrives in full sun and requires consistent moisture during its active growing period, while being tolerant of brief dry spells. It is not a heavy feeder, needing only low to moderate fertilization to support its growth in subtropical grassland conditions.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate fertilization. Benefits from a balanced, slow-release fertilizer if soil is poor, but generally not a heavy feeder.
