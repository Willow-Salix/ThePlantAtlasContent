## Care Guide
Brachymenium moss thrives in consistently moist, humid conditions with low to indirect light, similar to its native tropical and subtropical forest floor habitat. Ensure high ambient humidity and a consistently damp, but not soggy, substrate, avoiding direct sun. Fertilization is generally not necessary or should be extremely sparse and diluted.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilization is required; mosses generally have low nutrient demands and can be harmed by excessive salts. If desired, an extremely diluted liquid feed (e.g., 1/8th strength) could be applied very rarely.
