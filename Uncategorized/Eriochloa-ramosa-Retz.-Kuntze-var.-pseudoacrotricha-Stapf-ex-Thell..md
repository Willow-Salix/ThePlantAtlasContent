## Care Guide
This resilient grass thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moderate watering, with soil allowed to dry between sessions. It is quite drought-tolerant and requires only low to moderate fertilization during its active growth phases, adapting well to a variety of soil conditions.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. As a grass often found in disturbed or less fertile soils, it does not require heavy fertilization. A balanced, all-purpose fertilizer can be applied sparingly during the active growing season.
