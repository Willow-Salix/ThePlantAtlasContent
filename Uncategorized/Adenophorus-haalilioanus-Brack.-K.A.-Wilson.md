## Introduction
Adenophorus is a genus of ferns in the family Polypodiaceae, subfamily Grammitidoideae, according to the Pteridophyte Phylogeny Group classification of 2016 (PPG I). The genus is endemic to Hawaii.

## Care Guide
To care for the Island Kihifern, replicate its native Hawaiian tropical wet forest environment by providing bright indirect light and consistently moist conditions with very high humidity. Fertilize sparingly during its active growth period to support healthy development.

### Fertilizer
Light feeder. Apply a highly diluted liquid fertilizer (e.g., 1/4 strength) once a month during the active growing season (spring and summer).
