## ðŸ§¤ Care Instructions
The California Black Oak thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, characteristic of its Mediterranean climate. It requires consistent watering when young, gradually transitioning to drought-tolerant care with minimal summer irrigation once established. Fertilization is generally not necessary for mature trees.
