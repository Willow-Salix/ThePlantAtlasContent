Burclover thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, characteristic of its native Mediterranean climate. It requires moderate watering, tolerating periods of drought once established, and as a nitrogen-fixing legume, it has low fertilizer needs, especially for nitrogen.

Low to moderate. As a nitrogen-fixing legume, it generally does not require supplemental nitrogen. A balanced, low-nitrogen fertilizer can be applied if soil is very poor.
