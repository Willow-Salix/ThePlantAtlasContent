## Introduction
Clematis viorna, commonly known as vasevine or  leatherflower, is a flowering vine native to the southeastern United States. It grows in wooded habitats and bears purple flowers in spring and summer.

## Care Guide
Thriving in humid temperate zones, Vasevine requires full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil. Regular watering and a spring fertilization will support its vigorous growth and unique flowering.

### Fertilizer
Benefits from a balanced, all-purpose fertilizer in early spring as new growth begins. Thrives in moderately rich, humus-rich soil, typical of temperate woodlands.
