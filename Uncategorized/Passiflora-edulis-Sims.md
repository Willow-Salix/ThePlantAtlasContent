## Care Guide
The Purple Granadilla thrives in full sun and consistently moist, well-draining soil, mimicking its native tropical habitat. It is a heavy feeder, requiring regular fertilization to support its vigorous growth and abundant fruit production. Ensure adequate support for its climbing vines and protect it from frost.

### Fertilizer
A heavy feeder; requires regular fertilization with a balanced, all-purpose fertilizer during growth, switching to a high-potassium formula during flowering and fruiting.
