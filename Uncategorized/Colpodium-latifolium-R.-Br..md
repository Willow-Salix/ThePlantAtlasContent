## Care Guide
This arctic wetland grass thrives in consistently saturated soil conditions, mimicking its native boggy environment during the short growing season. Provide high light exposure and ensure cold tolerance, as it is adapted to nutrient-poor, frigid conditions with limited drainage.

### Fertilizer
Very low. Adapted to nutrient-poor, often acidic tundra soils; heavy fertilization is not necessary and may be detrimental.
