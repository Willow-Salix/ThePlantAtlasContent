## Introduction
Ribes cereum is a species of currant known by the common names wax currant and squaw currant; the pedicellare variety is known as whisky currant. The species is native to western North America.

## Care Guide
This drought-tolerant shrub thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, mirroring its native arid and mountainous habitat. Once established, it requires infrequent, deep watering and has low fertilizer needs, making it a low-maintenance plant.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate fertility needs; thrives in lean soils. A light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring is optional, particularly in very poor soils.
