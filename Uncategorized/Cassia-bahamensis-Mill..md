## Care Guide
Bahama Senna thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, mirroring its native tropical and subtropical dry forest environment. Provide regular watering during its active growth, ensuring the soil dries slightly between sessions, and apply a balanced fertilizer moderately. This species is relatively low-maintenance and drought-tolerant once established.

### Fertilizer
Moderate. A balanced, slow-release granular fertilizer applied once in spring and again in mid-summer during its active growing period. As a legume, it can fix some nitrogen, so heavy nitrogen fertilization is not typically needed.
