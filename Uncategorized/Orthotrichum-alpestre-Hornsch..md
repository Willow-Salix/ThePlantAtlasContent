## Care Guide
Orthotrichum alpestre thrives in cool, moist, high-humidity environments mimicking its native alpine habitat. Provide consistent moisture, but ensure good drainage to prevent waterlogging. It prefers filtered light or partial shade and requires very little, if any, supplemental fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Very low to no fertilizer. Mosses derive sufficient nutrients from their environment (rainwater, atmospheric deposition, minor organic decay).
