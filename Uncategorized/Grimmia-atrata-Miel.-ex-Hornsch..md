## Introduction
Grimmia is a genus of mosses, originally named by Jakob Friedrich Ehrhart in honour of Johann Friedrich Carl Grimm, a physician and botanist from Gotha, Germany.

## Care Guide
Grimmia Dry Rock Moss thrives in cool, high-altitude conditions with bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, mimicking its native alpine environment. Ensure excellent drainage to prevent waterlogging, and avoid heavy fertilization as it prefers nutrient-poor conditions.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Mosses are not heavy feeders, and alpine environments are typically nutrient-poor. High fertilizer concentrations can be detrimental.
