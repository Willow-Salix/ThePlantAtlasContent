## Care Guide
Pink Natal Grass thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, tolerating dry conditions well once established. Mimicking its native savanna environment, it requires regular watering during its active growing season, followed by drier periods. Fertilization needs are minimal.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate feeder. A single application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in spring is usually sufficient, or it may not require any in reasonably fertile soils.
