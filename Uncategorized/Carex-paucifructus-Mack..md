## Introduction
Carex praegracilis is a species of North American sedge known as clustered field sedge, field sedge, and expressway sedge. Carex praegracilis is cultivated in the specialty horticulture trade as lawn substitute and meadow-like plantings.

## Care Guide
Clustered Field Sedge, native to temperate North American grasslands and wetlands, prefers full sun to partial shade. It requires consistently moist soil, reflecting its natural habitat along streambanks and meadows, though it can tolerate short dry spells. This sedge generally thrives in average soil without the need for heavy fertilization.
