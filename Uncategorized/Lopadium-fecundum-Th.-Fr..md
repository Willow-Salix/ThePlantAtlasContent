## Care Guide
Lopadium fecundum is a lichen adapted to cool, humid boreal and subarctic environments. Its care requires stable temperatures, partial to filtered light, and high atmospheric moisture from clean air and precipitation. Avoid direct sun, air pollution, and any form of traditional fertilization as it can be detrimental.

### Fertilizer
Does not require traditional fertilizer. It obtains all necessary nutrients directly from airborne particles, dust, and precipitation. Highly sensitive to pollutants and chemical additives.
