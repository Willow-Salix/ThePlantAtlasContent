## Care Guide
Bluebill Clematis thrives in temperate climates with full sun to partial shade, requiring consistently moist, well-draining soil, particularly at the roots. It benefits from moderate fertilization in spring and is relatively low-maintenance once established, adapting well to its native woodland edge and prairie environments.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate needs. Apply a balanced, slow-release granular fertilizer or a layer of compost in early spring as new growth emerges. Avoid over-fertilization.
