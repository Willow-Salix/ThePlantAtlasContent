## Introduction

## Care Guide
This bulbous perennial thrives in conditions similar to its native temperate maritime coastal habitats. Provide full to partial sun exposure and ensure the soil remains consistently moist yet well-drained during its spring growth phase. Reduce watering and avoid heavy fertilization once the plant enters summer dormancy.

### Fertilizer
Light feeding with a balanced liquid fertilizer once or twice during the early spring growing season.
