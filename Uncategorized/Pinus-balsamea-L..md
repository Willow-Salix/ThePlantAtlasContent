## Introduction
Abies balsamea or balsam fir is a North American fir, native to most of eastern and central Canada (Newfoundland west to central Alberta) and the northeastern United States (Minnesota east to Maine, and south in the Appalachian Mountains to West Virginia).

## Care Guide
Balsam Fir thrives in cool, moist climates similar to its native boreal and northern temperate forests. It prefers full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained, acidic soil. Provide regular watering and minimal fertilization for optimal health.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer for evergreens can be applied sparingly in spring if soil nutrients are deficient, but often not needed in good soil.
