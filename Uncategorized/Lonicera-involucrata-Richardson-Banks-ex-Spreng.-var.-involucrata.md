## Introduction
Lonicera involucrata, the bearberry honeysuckle, bracted honeysuckle, twinberry honeysuckle, Californian Honeysuckle, twin-berry, or black twinberry, is a species of honeysuckle native to northern and western North America.

## Care Guide
Twinberry honeysuckle thrives in the temperate forests and riparian zones of Western North America, favoring consistently moist, well-drained soil and adaptable to partial to full sun. To ensure vigorous growth, provide frequent watering and a moderate application of balanced fertilizer or organic compost in spring.

### Fertilizer
Moderate, balanced fertilizer in early spring. Benefits greatly from the addition of organic matter or compost to the soil.
