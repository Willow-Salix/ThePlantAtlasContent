## Care Guide
California Goldenbanner thrives in sunny, well-drained locations, mirroring its native Mediterranean climate of California. It prefers moderate water during its active growth in spring, becoming quite drought-tolerant once established and requiring infrequent watering during dry summers. Due to its nitrogen-fixing nature, it generally requires minimal to no supplemental fertilization.
