## Care Guide
This Dudleya thrives in bright, direct sunlight with minimal watering, mimicking its arid coastal habitat. Ensure excellent drainage and water deeply but infrequently during its active fall-to-spring growth, avoiding water during its summer dormancy to prevent rot.

### Fertilizer
Low fertilizer needs. A diluted, balanced succulent fertilizer can be applied once or twice during the active growing season (fall-spring), but it is not essential for healthy growth.
