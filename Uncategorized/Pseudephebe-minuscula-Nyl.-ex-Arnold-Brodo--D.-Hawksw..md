## Care Guide
This alpine-arctic lichen thrives in very bright, cool conditions with high atmospheric humidity and excellent air circulation, mimicking its natural rock habitat. It requires no traditional watering or fertilization, instead absorbing moisture and trace nutrients directly from the environment. Maintaining cold, exposed, yet atmospherically moist conditions is crucial for its care.

### Fertilizer
No fertilizer is required. Lichens obtain nutrients from the air and their substrate and are highly sensitive to pollutants and excess nutrients, which can be detrimental to their growth.
