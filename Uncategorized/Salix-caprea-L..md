## Care Guide
Goat Willow is a robust temperate plant, best suited for full sun to partial shade in consistently moist, well-drained soil. It adapts to various soil conditions and requires low to moderate fertilization, making it a relatively easy-care choice.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. As a pioneer species, it can tolerate less fertile soils. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer in spring is typically sufficient if soil nutrients are poor.
