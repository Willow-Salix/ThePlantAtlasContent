## Care Guide
This terrestrial orchid thrives in conditions that mimic its native moist, shaded woodland habitats of Eastern North America. Provide consistent moisture to the growing medium, bright indirect light, and a very dilute orchid fertilizer during its active growth period. Ensure good air circulation and drainage to prevent fungal issues.

### Fertilizer
Light feeder. Apply a highly dilute, balanced orchid fertilizer (e.g., 1/4 to 1/8 strength) once every 2-4 weeks during the active growing season. Reduce or cease fertilization during dormancy.
