## Care Guide
This California native thrives in full sun and very well-drained, sandy soils, reflecting its coastal dune origins in a Mediterranean climate. It is highly drought-tolerant once established, requiring minimal watering, primarily during prolonged dry periods, and no supplemental fertilization. Ensure excellent drainage to prevent root rot.
