## Care Guide
This desert cactus thrives in conditions mimicking its arid native habitat: full sun, extremely well-draining soil, and infrequent watering. Overwatering is the most common cause of failure, especially during dormancy. Provide minimal fertilizer and protection from extreme cold.

### Fertilizer
Very low. Fertilize sparingly, if at all, with a weak, balanced, low-nitrogen fertilizer once or twice during the active growing season. Native soils are poor.
