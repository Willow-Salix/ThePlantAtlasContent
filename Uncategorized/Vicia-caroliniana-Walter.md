## Care Guide
Carolina Vetch is a low-maintenance plant adapted to temperate climates. It prefers full sun to partial shade and moderate watering with good drainage. As a nitrogen-fixing legume, it needs minimal to no supplemental fertilizer, thriving in average soil conditions.

### Fertilizer
As a legume (Fabaceae family), it fixes nitrogen and generally does not require supplemental nitrogen fertilizer. It thrives in average to poor soils; excessive fertilization can be detrimental.
