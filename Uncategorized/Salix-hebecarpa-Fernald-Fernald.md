## Care Guide
This bog willow thrives in constantly wet, acidic to neutral soils under full sun to partial shade, mimicking its native subarctic wetland habitat. Ensure high soil moisture is maintained, as it is intolerant of dry conditions, and provide light to moderate fertilization as necessary.

### Fertilizer
Light to moderate fertilization, especially if soil is naturally nutrient-poor. Prefers acidic to neutral soil.
