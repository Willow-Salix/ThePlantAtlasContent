## Introduction

It is known by the common names Utah solitaire lily, Intermountain false-wheatgrass, desert lily, lonely lily, and sand lily. It is native to the western United States, where it is known from Arizona and Utah.

## Care Guide
The Lonely Lily thrives in conditions that mimic its native arid desert environment. Provide full, intense sun and very infrequent, deep watering, allowing the soil to completely dry out between applications. Minimal fertilization is required due to its adaptation to lean desert soils.

### Fertilizer
Very low to no fertilizer needed; native desert soils are typically lean. If desired, a highly diluted, balanced fertilizer can be applied sparingly during active growth.
