## Care Guide
Buckeyes prefer conditions mirroring their native temperate forest environments, requiring full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil. Regular watering during dry spells is crucial, and they benefit from fertile ground, ideally enriched with organic matter. Providing these conditions ensures robust growth and overall plant health.

### Fertilizer
Thrives in fertile, organically rich soil. Benefits from an annual application of balanced, slow-release fertilizer or compost in early spring.
