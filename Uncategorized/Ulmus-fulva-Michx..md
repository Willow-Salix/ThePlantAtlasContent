## Introduction
Ulmus rubra, the slippery elm, is a species of elm native to eastern North America.
Other common names include red elm, gray elm, soft elm, moose elm, and Indian elm.

Slippery Elm prefers a temperate deciduous forest environment, needing full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, fertile soil. Regular watering is crucial, and providing adequate nutrients will support its robust growth.
