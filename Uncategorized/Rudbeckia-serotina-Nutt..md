## Care Guide
This resilient North American native flourishes in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering but tolerating drought once established. It is not a heavy feeder, thriving in average to poor soil conditions without the need for extensive fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Thrives in average to poor soils and does not typically require rich fertilization. An annual top-dressing of compost can be beneficial.
