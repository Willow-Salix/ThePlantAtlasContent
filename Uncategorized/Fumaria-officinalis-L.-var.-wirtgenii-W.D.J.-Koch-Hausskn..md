Fumaria officinalis, the common fumitory, drug fumitory or earth smoke, is a herbaceous annual flowering plant in the poppy family Papaveraceae. It is the most common species of the genus Fumaria in Western and Central Europe.

## Care Guide
Common Fumitory is an adaptable plant that thrives in full sun to partial shade in temperate environments. It prefers moderately moist, well-draining soil but is quite tolerant of average conditions. Mimicking its natural habitat in open, disturbed ground with minimal fertilization will ensure its success.
