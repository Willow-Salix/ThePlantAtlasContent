## Care Guide
To thrive, New Mexico maple needs consistently moist, well-drained soil and a location with partial shade to full sun, adapting best to full sun in cooler climates. Provide regular watering, especially during dry spells, and consider a light annual fertilization for optimal growth. Mimic its native montane riparian habitat for best results.

### Fertilizer
Moderate fertility; a light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring can be beneficial, particularly in lean soils.
