## Care Guide
Bramblefern thrives in a warm, humid environment with bright indirect to partial shade. Keep the soil consistently moist, allowing only the very top layer to dry out slightly between waterings. Fertilize lightly during the active growing season.

### Fertilizer
Light feeder. Fertilize sparingly (e.g., monthly to bi-monthly) during the growing season with a half-strength balanced liquid fertilizer.
