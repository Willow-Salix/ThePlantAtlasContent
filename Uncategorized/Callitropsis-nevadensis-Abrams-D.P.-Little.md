## Care Guide
This drought-tolerant cypress thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, characteristic of its native Californian arid scrubland. Water deeply but infrequently, allowing the soil to dry completely between waterings, and fertilize very sparingly if at all, as it's adapted to nutrient-poor conditions.
