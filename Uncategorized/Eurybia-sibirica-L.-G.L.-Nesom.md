## Care Guide
Arctic Aster thrives in cool climates with full to partial sun exposure, mimicking its open native habitat. It requires consistently moist but very well-drained soil, as it adapted to conditions of melting snow and ice in well-drained rocky areas. Fertilization should be kept minimal due to its adaptation to nutrient-poor tundra and boreal soils.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Native tundra and boreal soils are typically poor in nutrients. Over-fertilization can be detrimental; a light annual application of a balanced fertilizer or compost in spring is sufficient if the soil is particularly lean.
