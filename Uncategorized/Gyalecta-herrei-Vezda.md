## Introduction
Gyalecta is a genus of lichen-forming fungi in the family Gyalectaceae that contains 50 species. Gyalecta was circumscribed by lichenologist Erik Acharius in 1808. It forms associations with Trentepohlia algae.

## Care Guide
Herre's Dimple Lichen thrives in cool, highly humid conditions with indirect light, replicating its native temperate forest floor habitat. ### Fertilizer
None. Lichens are self-sufficient in obtaining nutrients from the air and rain, and supplemental fertilization can be harmful.
