## Care Guide
Fabronia moss thrives in consistently moist, highly humid conditions and prefers partial to full shade, mimicking its native forest understory habitat. Regular misting is essential to maintain surface moisture, and direct sunlight should be avoided. Due to its low nutrient needs, fertilization should be minimal or absent.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to none. Mosses generally have low nutrient demands and can be sensitive to high fertilizer concentrations. If used, apply a very diluted, specialized moss or orchid fertilizer infrequently.
