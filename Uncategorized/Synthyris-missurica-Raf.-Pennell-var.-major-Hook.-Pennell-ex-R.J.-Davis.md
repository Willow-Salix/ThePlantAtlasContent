## Care Guide
Missouri Synthyris thrives in cool, moist, subalpine conditions with dappled sunlight. Provide consistently moist, well-drained soil rich in organic matter. Protect from intense afternoon sun and ensure regular watering to replicate its native forest and meadow habitat.

### Fertilizer
Benefits from rich, humusy soil. Incorporate organic matter like compost into the soil. A light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in spring can be beneficial, but heavy feeding is not necessary.
