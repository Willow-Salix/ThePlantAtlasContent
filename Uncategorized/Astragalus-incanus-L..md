## Care Guide
Astragalus incanus thrives in full sun and well-draining, dry soil, mimicking its native European dry grasslands and rocky hillsides. It is highly drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering once established, preferring its soil to dry out between waterings. Fertilization is rarely needed due to its nitrogen-fixing ability and adaptation to lean, calcareous soils.

### Fertilizer
