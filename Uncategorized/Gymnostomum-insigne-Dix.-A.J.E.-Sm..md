## Care Guide
This calcicolous moss thrives in cool, damp, and shaded conditions, mimicking its native temperate maritime microclimates. Ensure constant moisture in the substrate without waterlogging, and provide high humidity. Avoid direct sunlight and refrain from heavy fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Mosses have very low nutrient demands and can be harmed by excessive or concentrated feeding.
