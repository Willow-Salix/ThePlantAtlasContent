## Introduction
Arctotheca calendula is a plant in the sunflower family commonly known as capeweed, plain treasureflower, cape dandelion, or cape marigold because it occurs in the Western Cape Province in South Africa. It has a wide distribution in coastal areas of South Africa, extending from Namaqualand to Cape Point and as far as KwaZulu-Natal.

## Care Guide
Capeweed, native to the Mediterranean climate of South Africa, thrives in full sun and is highly drought-tolerant once established. It requires moderate watering during active growth but should be allowed to dry out between waterings. This plant is not a heavy feeder and can prosper in average soils with minimal fertilization.
