## Introduction

## Care Guide
Rimelia simulans thrives in humid environments with moderate, indirect light, mimicking its native subtropical and tropical forest habitats. Maintain high humidity and provide regular moisture through misting or simulated rainfall, ensuring good air circulation. Fertilizers are not needed and can be harmful to this delicate lichen.

### Fertilizer
None required. Lichens derive nutrients from the air and rainwater; conventional fertilizers are harmful and should be strictly avoided.
