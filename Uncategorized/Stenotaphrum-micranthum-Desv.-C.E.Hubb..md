## Introduction
Stenotaphrum is a widespread genus of plants in the grass family.
The name is derived from the Greek words στενός (stenós), meaning "narrow", and τάφρος (táphros), meaning "trench". It refers to cavities in the raceme axis.

## Care Guide
This tropical grass thrives in full sun to partial shade, preferring consistently moist, well-drained soil. Regular watering and moderate to high fertilization are key to maintaining its dense, healthy growth, particularly in warmer climates.

### Fertilizer
