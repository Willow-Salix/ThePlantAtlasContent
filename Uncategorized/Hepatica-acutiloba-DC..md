## Introduction
Hepatica (hepatica, liverleaf, or liverwort) is a genus of herbaceous perennials in the buttercup family, native to central and northern Europe, Asia and eastern North America. Some botanists include Hepatica within a wider interpretation of Anemone.

## Care Guide
Sharp-lobed Hepatica thrives in a temperate deciduous forest environment, requiring partial to full shade once trees leaf out and consistently moist, well-drained, organically rich soil. Mimic its native habitat by providing adequate moisture in spring and allowing for a period of drier soil during its summer dormancy.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; benefits from rich, organic soil or an annual top-dressing of compost/leaf mold in early spring.
