## Care Guide
Orange Wall Lichen thrives in well-lit, exposed environments typical of temperate regions, requiring no soil or conventional watering. Provide regular exposure to moisture (rain, mist) followed by dry periods. This hardy species needs no fertilizer, acquiring all nutrients from its environment.
