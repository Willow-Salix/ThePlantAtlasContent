## Introduction
Cyperus bipartitus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, river cyperus, or the shining flatsedge, is a common species of sedge. The name "slender flatsedge" also applies to Cyperus gracilis.

## Care Guide
This wetland plant requires consistently wet to moist soil, mimicking its native habitat. Provide full sun to partial shade and fertilize moderately during its active growth to ensure optimal health.

### Fertilizer
Moderate; apply a balanced liquid fertilizer at half strength once every 2-4 weeks during the growing season.
