## Care Guide
Coiled Lousewort thrives in cool, moist conditions reminiscent of its native subalpine meadows. Provide full sun to partial shade with consistently moist, well-drained soil. Fertilization should be kept minimal, as it is adapted to leaner soils and is hemiparasitic.
