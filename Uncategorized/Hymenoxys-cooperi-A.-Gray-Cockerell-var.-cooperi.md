## Introduction
Hymenoxys (rubberweed or bitterweed) is a genus of plants in the sunflower family, native to North and South America. It was named by Alexandre Henri Gabriel de Cassini in 1828.
Plants of this genus are toxic to sheep due to the presence of the sesquiterpene lactone hymenoxon.

## Care Guide
Cooper's Rubberweed is a resilient, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and lean, well-draining soils, mimicking its native arid montane environment. Provide infrequent, deep watering and avoid rich soils or heavy fertilization to ensure optimal health and prevent root rot.

### Fertilizer
Thrives in lean, well-draining soils and generally requires minimal to no fertilization. If desired, a very diluted, low-nitrogen fertilizer can be applied once in spring.
