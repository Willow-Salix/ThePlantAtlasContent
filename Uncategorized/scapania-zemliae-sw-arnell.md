## ðŸ§¤ Care Instructions
This Arctic liverwort thrives in consistently cool temperatures, high humidity, and a continuously moist, but not waterlogged, substrate. Provide low to indirect light, mimicking its sheltered tundra habitat, and avoid any direct sun or dry conditions. Fertilization is rarely needed.

### ðŸ§ª Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Tundra environments are nutrient-poor, and liverworts are adapted to low-nutrient conditions. If growth seems stagnant, a highly diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer can be applied very infrequently.
