## Care Guide
This rock-dwelling moss thrives with bright indirect to partial direct sun exposure, provided high humidity is maintained. Keep its substrate consistently damp but ensure excellent drainage, and avoid over-fertilization as it prefers nutrient-poor environments.

### Fertilizer
Very minimal to no fertilization needed, as mosses thrive in nutrient-poor conditions.
