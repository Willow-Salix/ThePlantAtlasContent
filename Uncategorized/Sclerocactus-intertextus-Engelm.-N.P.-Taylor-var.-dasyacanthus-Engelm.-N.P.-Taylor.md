## Introduction

## Care Guide
This desert cactus thrives in full sun and requires very infrequent watering, allowing the soil to dry thoroughly between sessions to prevent rot. Fertilize sparingly with a low-nitrogen formula during its brief growing period in spring and early summer. Ensure excellent drainage and protect from excessive moisture, especially in cooler temperatures.

### Fertilizer
Minimal fertilization. Use a low-nitrogen, high-phosphorus/potassium cactus fertilizer once or twice during the active growing season (spring to early summer).
