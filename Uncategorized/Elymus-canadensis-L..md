## Care Guide
Canada Wildrye is a hardy native grass requiring full sun and well-drained soil. It is very drought-tolerant once established and thrives with minimal watering and no supplemental fertilization. Its adaptability makes it a low-maintenance choice for various temperate landscapes.

### Fertilizer
Low; adapted to average to poor soils. Generally requires no fertilization in garden settings, as excessive nutrients can lead to lush but weak growth.
