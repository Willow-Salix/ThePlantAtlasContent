## Introduction
Ceratopteris is the only genus among homosporous ferns that is exclusively aquatic. It is pan-tropical and classified in the Parkerioideae subfamily of the family Pteridaceae.

## Care Guide
Ceratopteris gaudichaudii is an aquatic fern requiring constant moisture, either fully submerged or with its roots consistently wet. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and benefits from regular nutrient supplementation. Mimic its native wetland environment for optimal growth.

### Fertilizer
Moderate to high nutrient needs. Benefits from liquid fertilizers in the water column (for submerged growth) or a nutrient-rich substrate (for emersed growth).
