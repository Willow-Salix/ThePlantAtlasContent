## Care Guide
This liverwort thrives in conditions mimicking its native shaded, humid forest floor. Provide very low, indirect light and maintain consistently high humidity and moisture in the substrate without waterlogging. Fertilization is generally unnecessary.

### Fertilizer
Extremely minimal to none; liverworts absorb nutrients from their environment and rainwater, and excess fertilizer can be detrimental.
