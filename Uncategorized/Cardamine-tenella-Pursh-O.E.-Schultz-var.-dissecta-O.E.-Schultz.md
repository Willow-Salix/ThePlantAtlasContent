## Care Guide
This spring ephemeral thrives in the dappled light and moist, rich soil of a temperate deciduous forest floor, completing its growth cycle rapidly in early spring before the tree canopy fully develops. It requires consistent moisture during its active period and enters a dormant phase in summer.

### Fertilizer
Prefers rich, organic soil. Amend with compost or a balanced slow-release fertilizer in early spring.
