## Introduction
Hypolepis (beadfern) is a genus of ferns described as a genus in 1806. The word is derived from Greek, meaning "under scale". It is found in tropical and subtropical regions, primarily in the New World but also in the Old World and on various oceanic islands.

## Care Guide
Beadferns thrive in conditions that mimic their native forest floor habitat: consistently moist soil, high humidity, and dappled shade. Protect from direct sun and provide regular, but not excessive, fertilization during growth. Ensure good air circulation to prevent fungal issues.

### Fertilizer
Moderate feeding with a balanced liquid fertilizer, diluted to half strength, every 2-4 weeks during the active growing season. Reduce or cease in winter.
