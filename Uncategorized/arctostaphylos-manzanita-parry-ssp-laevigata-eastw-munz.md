## ðŸ§¤ Care Instructions
Contra Costa manzanita flourishes in full sun and well-draining, lean soil, mirroring its native Mediterranean chaparral habitat. Once established, it is highly drought-tolerant and should receive minimal to no summer irrigation to prevent root rot. Fertilizer is generally unnecessary and can be harmful due to its adaptation to low-nutrient environments.
