## Care Guide
Orthotrichum moss thrives in bright indirect to partial shade conditions typical of temperate woodlands. It requires consistent moisture and high humidity, often benefiting from regular misting. Fertilization is generally not necessary, reflecting its natural habitat on nutrient-poor surfaces like tree bark and rocks.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer; if needed, use a highly diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer very sparingly once or twice a year during active growth.
