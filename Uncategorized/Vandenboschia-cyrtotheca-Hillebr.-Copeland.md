## Care Guide
This delicate Hawaiian Filmy Fern thrives in consistently moist, very high humidity conditions with extremely low light, mirroring its native rainforest understory habitat. Ensure constant moisture and protect from any direct sun for successful cultivation.

### Fertilizer
Minimal. Use a highly diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer (1/8 to 1/4 strength) very sparingly, perhaps once every 2-3 months during active growth periods.
