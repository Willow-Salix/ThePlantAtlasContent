## Care Guide
This plant thrives in consistently moist to wet soil, mirroring its native wetland habitat. It prefers full sun to partial shade, adapting well to various light conditions as long as its high moisture needs are met. Minimal fertilization is required, making it a relatively low-maintenance option for appropriate sites.

### Fertilizer
