## Introduction
Cenchrus ciliaris (buffel-grass or African foxtail grass; syn. Pennisetum ciliare (L.) Link) is a species of grass native to most of Africa, southern Asia (east to India), southern Iran, and the extreme south of Europe (Sicily). Other names by which this grass is known include dhaman grass, anjan grass and koluk katai.

## Care Guide
Buffelgrass is an incredibly resilient plant best suited for full sun exposure, mimicking its native semi-arid savanna habitat. It is highly drought-tolerant, demanding infrequent watering once established, and thrives with low to moderate nutrient input. This plant is ideal for xeriscaping or areas with limited water availability.
