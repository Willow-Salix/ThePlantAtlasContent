## Introduction
Hackelia virginiana, a biennial plant, is commonly known as beggar's lice, sticktight or stickseed.  However, the common names beggar's lice and stick-tight are also used for very different plants, such as Desmodium species that are also known as "tick-trefoil".

## Care Guide
To care for Beggarslice, provide a location with partial to full shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil rich in organic matter. Water regularly to prevent drying out, but ensure good drainage. Fertilization is generally minimal, benefiting from an annual addition of compost or a light, balanced fertilizer.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate fertilization. Prefers soils rich in organic matter. A light application of balanced, slow-release fertilizer or compost annually in spring should suffice.
