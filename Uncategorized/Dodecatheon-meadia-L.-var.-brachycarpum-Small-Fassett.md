## Care Guide
This spring ephemeral requires full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-draining soil during its active growth phase in spring. After flowering, it enters dormancy, needing a dry period through summer. Plant in rich, organic soil and fertilize sparingly.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Thrives in rich, well-draining soil with ample organic matter. A light application of balanced, slow-release fertilizer or compost in early spring is usually sufficient.
