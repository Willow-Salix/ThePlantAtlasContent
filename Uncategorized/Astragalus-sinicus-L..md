## Care Guide
Chinese milk-vetch thrives in full sun and consistently moist soil, reflecting its native temperate monsoon climate. As a nitrogen-fixing legume, it requires minimal fertilization and naturally improves soil fertility.

### Fertilizer
Low, as a nitrogen-fixing legume it enriches the soil; a low-nitrogen, balanced fertilizer can be applied if soil is exceptionally poor.
