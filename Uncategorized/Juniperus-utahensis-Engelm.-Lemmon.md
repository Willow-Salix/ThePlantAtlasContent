## Care Guide
The Utah Juniper is an exceptionally drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining, poor soils. It requires minimal watering once established and generally does not need fertilization. Mimic its native arid environment for best results.

### Fertilizer
Low to no fertilizer required; thrives in nutrient-poor, rocky, or sandy soils. Over-fertilization can be detrimental.
