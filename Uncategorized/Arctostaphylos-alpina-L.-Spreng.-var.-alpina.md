## Introduction
Arctous alpina (syn. Arctostaphylos alpina), the alpine bearberry, mountain bearberry or black bearberry, is a dwarf shrub in the heather family Ericaceae. The basionym of this species is Arbutus alpina L..

## Care Guide
To care for Alpine Bearberry, mimic its native alpine tundra conditions by providing full sun and well-draining, acidic soil. Ensure consistent, moderate moisture during its growing season, but absolutely avoid waterlogging. Fertilization is generally not required and can be harmful.

### Fertilizer
Very low to no fertilizer. This plant is adapted to poor, lean, acidic, and well-drained soils typical of alpine environments; over-fertilization can be detrimental.
