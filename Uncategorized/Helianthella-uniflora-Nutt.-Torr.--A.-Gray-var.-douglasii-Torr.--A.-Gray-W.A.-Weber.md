## Care Guide
Douglas' Helianthella thrives in well-drained soil under full to partial sun, mimicking its native montane meadow and woodland edge habitat. Provide moderate watering during its active growth period, allowing for drier conditions once the plant is established. It requires minimal fertilizer, adapting well to average soil conditions.
