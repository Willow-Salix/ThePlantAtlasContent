## Care Guide
The Forked Lineleaf Fern thrives in conditions mimicking its native tropical rainforest. Provide low to indirect light, maintain consistently moist substrate alongside high humidity, and fertilize lightly during its active growth period.

### Fertilizer
Light fertilization is sufficient; a diluted liquid fertilizer (e.g., half-strength) can be applied monthly or bi-monthly during its active growing season. Being epiphytic/lithophytic, it's not a heavy feeder.
