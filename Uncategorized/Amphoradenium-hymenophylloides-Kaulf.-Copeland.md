## Care Guide
This delicate filmy fern thrives in conditions mimicking its native tropical rainforest and cloud forest habitats: consistently high humidity, constant moisture, and very low light. It requires a humid enclosure like a terrarium to prevent desiccation and minimal fertilization due to its sensitivity.

### Fertilizer
Very minimal to none. If fertilized, use a highly diluted (1/8 to 1/10 strength) liquid fertilizer sparingly, perhaps once a month during active growth. These plants are sensitive to salt build-up.
