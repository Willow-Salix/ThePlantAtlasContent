## Care Guide
This hawthorn thrives in temperate conditions, preferring full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil. It's adaptable and can tolerate some drought once established, requiring only light to moderate fertilization to support its growth in most garden settings.

### Fertilizer
Light to moderate feeding. Benefits from an annual application of compost or a balanced slow-release fertilizer in spring, particularly in poor soils.
