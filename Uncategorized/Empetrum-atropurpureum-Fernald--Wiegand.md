## Introduction
Empetrum is a genus of three species of dwarf evergreen shrubs in the heath family, Ericaceae.  They are commonly known as crowberries and bear edible fruit. Species of Empetrum include: E. nigrum (crowberry) and its tetraploid form, sometimes given its own name hermaphroditum, E. eamesii and E. rubrum.

## Care Guide
Purple Crowberry thrives in cool, subalpine conditions with full to partial sun. It requires consistently moist, well-drained, acidic soil and does not need heavy fertilization. Protect from excessive heat and humidity.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer; adapted to lean, acidic, and nutrient-poor soils.
