## Care Guide
This epiphytic fern thrives in conditions replicating its native tropical cloud forest habitat, demanding consistently moist, well-draining substrate and very high humidity. Provide low to medium indirect light and fertilize sparingly to support its growth.

### Fertilizer
Light feeding is sufficient, typically once a month during the active growing season (spring/summer) using a highly diluted liquid fertilizer (e.g., 1/4 strength). Epiphytic ferns derive most nutrients from decaying organic matter and are not heavy feeders.
