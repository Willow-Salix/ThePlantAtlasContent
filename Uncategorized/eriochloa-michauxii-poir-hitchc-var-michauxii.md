This longleaf cupgrass thrives in the full sun and humid conditions characteristic of the southeastern United States. Provide consistent moisture, allowing the soil to dry slightly between waterings, and it can tolerate some drought. Fertilization is generally light, making it a relatively low-maintenance grass.
