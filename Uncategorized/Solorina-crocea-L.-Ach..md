## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in cool, humid conditions with bright, indirect light, much like its native alpine and arctic habitats. Maintaining high environmental moisture through frequent misting is crucial, and traditional fertilizers should be avoided as they can be detrimental. The key is to replicate a stable, cool, and damp microclimate.

### Fertilizer
No fertilizer is required or recommended; lichens absorb all necessary nutrients directly from the air and precipitation, and conventional fertilizers can be detrimental.
