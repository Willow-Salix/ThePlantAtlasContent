## Care Guide
Pylais' orthotrichum moss requires a cool, humid environment with consistent moisture and excellent drainage. Provide partial shade to bright, indirect light. Fertilization is generally not needed, as excessive nutrients can be detrimental to its growth.

### Fertilizer
Very low to none. Mosses are non-vascular and derive most of their minimal nutrient needs from rainwater, dust, and decaying organic matter on their substrate. Excessive fertilization can be harmful.
