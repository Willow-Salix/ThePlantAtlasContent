## Introduction
Populus deltoides, the eastern cottonwood or necklace poplar, is a species of cottonwood poplar native to North America, growing throughout the eastern, central, and southwestern United States as well as the southern Canadian prairies, the southernmost part of eastern Canada, and northeastern Mexico.

## Care Guide
This fast-growing tree thrives in full sun and consistently moist, well-drained soil, mimicking its native riparian habitats. Ensure adequate water, especially when young, and provide ample space for its large growth.

### Fertilizer
Generally low. Can benefit from a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in poor soils or for young, establishing trees. Established trees rarely require fertilization.
