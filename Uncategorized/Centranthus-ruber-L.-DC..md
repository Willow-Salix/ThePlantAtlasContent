## Introduction
Valeriana rubra (synonym Centranthus ruber), the red valerian, spur valerian, kiss-me-quick, fox's brush, devil's beard or Jupiter's beard, is a popular garden plant grown for its ornamental flowers.

## Care Guide
Red Valerian is a low-maintenance plant that prefers full sun and well-drained, lean soils. Once established, it is highly drought-tolerant, requiring infrequent watering, and generally benefits from little to no fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Very low. This plant thrives in poor to average soils and typically does not require supplemental fertilizer. Over-fertilizing can lead to leggy growth and reduced flowering.
