## Care Guide
This resilient moss thrives in high light conditions on exposed, dry rocks in mountainous, arid environments. It requires infrequent but complete watering to mimic natural precipitation patterns, tolerating extended dry periods, and has extremely low nutrient needs.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer required. Mosses derive nutrients from airborne dust, rainwater, and the substrate itself, making additional fertilization unnecessary and potentially detrimental.
