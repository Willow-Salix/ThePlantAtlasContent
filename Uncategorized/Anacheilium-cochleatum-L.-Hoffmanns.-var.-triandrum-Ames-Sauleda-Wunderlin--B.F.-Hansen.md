## Care Guide
This orchid requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, allowing the roots to dry slightly between waterings. High humidity is preferred, mimicking its tropical forest home. Fertilize sparingly during active growth to support its epiphytic nature.

### Fertilizer
Fertilize lightly with a balanced orchid-specific fertilizer, diluted to half or quarter strength, every 2-4 weeks during the active growing season. Reduce or stop fertilizing during dormancy.
