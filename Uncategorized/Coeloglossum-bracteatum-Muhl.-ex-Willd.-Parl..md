## Care Guide
This temperate orchid thrives in partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil during its active growing season. Provide bright, indirect light and a dilute, balanced fertilizer during spring and summer. Ensure a period of reduced watering and cooler temperatures during dormancy in winter.

### Fertilizer
Light feeder; use a dilute, balanced liquid fertilizer (e.g., 1/4 to 1/2 strength) every 2-4 weeks during active growth. Cease fertilization during dormancy.
