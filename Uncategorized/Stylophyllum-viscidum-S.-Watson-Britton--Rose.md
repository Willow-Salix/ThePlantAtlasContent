This succulent thrives in full sun, mimicking its native Mediterranean scrubland habitat. Water deeply but infrequently during its active growth from fall to spring, allowing the soil to dry out completely, and keep very dry during its summer dormancy. Minimal fertilization is needed due to its adaptation to nutrient-poor soils.
