## Care Guide
This tropical moss thrives in a consistently humid environment with very low to partial light, mimicking its native forest floor habitat. Ensure the substrate remains moist but not waterlogged, and provide high ambient humidity through regular misting or a terrarium. Minimal fertilization is required.

### Fertilizer
Very low nutrient needs. If fertilized, use a very dilute solution (e.g., 1/4 strength or less) infrequently, perhaps once a month during active growth.
