## Care Guide
The Syrian pea thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, characteristic of its Mediterranean habitat. Water moderately during its active growing season, allowing the soil to dry completely between applications, and reduce watering significantly during its summer dormancy. Given its nitrogen-fixing ability, it requires minimal fertilization.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. As a member of the Fabaceae family, it is a nitrogen-fixer, meaning it enriches the soil with nitrogen. Supplemental nitrogen fertilizer is generally not required; a balanced, low-dose fertilizer or compost can be applied sparingly if soil appears poor.
