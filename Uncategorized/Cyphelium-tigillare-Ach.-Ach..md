## Introduction
Cyphelium is a genus of crustose areolate lichens with cup-like apothecia filled with sooty black spores. The genus is in the family Caliciaceae . The genus has a widespread distribution, especially in north and south temperate regions, and contains about 12 species. Members of the genus are commonly called soot lichens.

## Care Guide
Soot Lichen thrives in bright indirect to partial sun conditions, replicating its natural habitat on sun-exposed deadwood in temperate and boreal forests. It requires high ambient humidity and occasional misting for moisture absorption, rather than traditional watering. No specific fertilization is needed for its growth.
