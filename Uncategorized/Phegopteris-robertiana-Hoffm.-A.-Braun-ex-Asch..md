## Care Guide
To mimic its native temperate forest understory, provide ample shade and maintain consistently moist, well-draining soil. This fern thrives with high humidity and minimal fertilization, reflecting its adaptation to nutrient-cycling from organic matter on limestone-rich substrates.

### Fertilizer
Light fertilization is sufficient. In its native environment, it derives nutrients from decaying organic matter. Apply a diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer sparingly, perhaps once or twice during the growing season (spring and summer).
