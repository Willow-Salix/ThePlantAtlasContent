## Care Guide
To mimic its native cool, moist forest habitat, provide this plant with partial to full sun, ensuring it receives afternoon shade in warmer areas. Maintain consistently moist, organically rich soil and water regularly, particularly during dry spells.

### Fertilizer
Moderate fertility. Thrives in organically rich, well-draining soil. Annual amendment with compost or a balanced slow-release fertilizer is beneficial.
