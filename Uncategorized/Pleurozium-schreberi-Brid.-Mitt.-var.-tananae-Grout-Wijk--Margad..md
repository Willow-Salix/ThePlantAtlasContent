## Care Guide
To care for this moss, provide a consistently shady or indirectly lit environment with high humidity. Maintain a constantly moist substrate without waterlogging, and avoid applying any significant amount of fertilizer.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer needed. Mosses are adapted to low-nutrient environments and can be sensitive to high concentrations of salts found in typical fertilizers.
