## Care Guide
Claytonia triphylla thrives in consistently moist conditions during its spring growth, mimicking its native subalpine meadows. Provide bright indirect light to partial sun and a light feeding during this active phase. It is an ephemeral plant, often going dormant and requiring less water during summer.

### Fertilizer
