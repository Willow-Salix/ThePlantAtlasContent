## Introduction
Clematis recta, the erect climaxis or ground virgins, is a species of Clematis unusual in that it is a free-standing shrub rather than a climbing plant.  Growing usually on the margins of woodland areas, it is native to Eastern, Southern and Mid Europe.

## Care Guide
Originating from temperate European grasslands and dry woodlands, this plant thrives in full sun to light shade and requires well-drained, moderately fertile soil. Water consistently, allowing for slight drying between sessions, and provide a balanced fertilizer during its growing season to encourage robust flowering.

### Fertilizer
Moderate, balanced fertilizer applied in spring and mid-summer, or an annual dressing of compost, to support its growth and flowering.
