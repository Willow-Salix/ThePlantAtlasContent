## Introduction
Tanacetum balsamita is a perennial temperate herb known as costmary, alecost, balsam herb, bible leaf, or mint geranium. A fragrant plant native to southern Europe and western Asia, it has been used over centuries for culinary, aromatic, and traditional medicine purposes.

## Care Guide
This plant, commonly known as Costmary, thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, reflecting its origins in temperate dry grasslands. It prefers moderate to low watering, tolerating dry conditions once established. Fertilization should be kept minimal to avoid overgrowth and maintain its aromatic qualities.
