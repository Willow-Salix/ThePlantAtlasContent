## Care Guide
Adapted to dry summers and wet winters, the Chaparral Currant prefers full sun to partial shade and is highly drought-tolerant once established. Provide moderate water during its spring growth and fruiting period, ensuring well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

### Fertilizer
Light, balanced organic fertilizer annually in early spring; thrives in average to lean, well-draining soils.
