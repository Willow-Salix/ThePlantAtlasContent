## Introduction
Citrus × sinensis (sometimes written Citrus sinensis), is a broadleaf evergreen hybrid between pomelo (Citrus maxima) and mandarin (Citrus reticulata). Hybrid sweet oranges are commonly cultivated fruits that include Valencia oranges, blood oranges and navel oranges.

## Care Guide
Sweet oranges thrive in full sun with consistent watering and good drainage, mimicking their subtropical native environment. They require moderate to high fertilization, especially when actively growing and bearing fruit, to ensure robust health and abundant yields.

### Fertilizer
Moderate to high, particularly during active growth and fruiting seasons, using a balanced citrus-specific fertilizer.
