## Care Guide
Snow peas thrive in full sun with consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Due to nitrogen fixation, they require minimal nitrogen fertilizer, focusing instead on phosphorus and potassium. Regular watering, particularly during flowering, is crucial for good pod development.

### Fertilizer
Low nitrogen requirements due to their ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen. Benefits from moderate phosphorus and potassium. Excessive nitrogen can promote leafy growth at the expense of pod production.
