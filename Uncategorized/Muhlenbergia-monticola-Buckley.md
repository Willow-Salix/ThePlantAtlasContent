## Care Guide
Mountain Muhly is a resilient, low-maintenance grass that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, typical of its semi-arid montane origins. Water deeply but infrequently, allowing soil to dry thoroughly, and it requires minimal fertilization, making it ideal for xeriscaping or dry garden areas.

### Fertilizer
Low to minimal. Soils in its native habitat are often lean and rocky; therefore, heavy fertilization is unnecessary and can be detrimental. A light, slow-release granular fertilizer once in spring, if at all, is sufficient.
