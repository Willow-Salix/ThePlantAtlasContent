## Introduction
Thelotrema is a genus of lichen-forming fungi in the family Graphidaceae, the family to which all taxa in the former Thelotremataceae now belong.
Members of the genus Thelotrema are commonly called barnacle lichens.

## Care Guide
Provide a consistently humid environment with indirect or dappled light, replicating its native forest understory conditions. Do not use conventional fertilizers, as lichens absorb moisture and nutrients directly from the air and substrate, making them sensitive to chemical inputs.

### Fertilizer
No traditional fertilizer is required. Lichens obtain necessary trace nutrients from air and rainfall; chemical fertilizers are often detrimental.
