## Introduction
Amsinckia spectabilis is a species of fiddleneck known by the common names seaside fiddleneck and woolly breeches. It is native to the west coast of North America from British Columbia to Baja California, where it grows in sandy habitat, including direct coastline.

## Care Guide
Seaside Fiddleneck thrives in full sun, reflecting its native coastal exposure. It prefers well-draining soil and is quite drought-tolerant once established, requiring infrequent watering, especially after its main growth period. Fertilization is generally not necessary, as it adapts well to lean soils.

### Fertilizer
