## Care Guide
This widespread moss thrives in consistently moist, shaded to partially shaded environments, typical of temperate and subalpine regions. It requires high humidity and regular watering to prevent drying out, with minimal to no fertilizer necessary for healthy growth.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer needed. Mosses are not heavy feeders and can be harmed by excess nutrients. If used, apply a very dilute, balanced liquid fertilizer sparingly, once or twice a year at most.
