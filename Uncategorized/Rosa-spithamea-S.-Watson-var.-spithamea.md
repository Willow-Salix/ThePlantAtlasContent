## Care Guide
The Ground Rose prefers conditions similar to its native California habitat, thriving in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil. It is drought-tolerant once established, requiring moderate water during dry periods and light fertilization. Ensure good air circulation to promote health and vigorous growth.

### Fertilizer
Light to moderate feeding. Can benefit from a balanced, all-purpose fertilizer applied in early spring, but generally does well in lean, well-drained soils without heavy fertilization.
