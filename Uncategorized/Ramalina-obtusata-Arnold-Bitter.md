## Introduction
Ramalina is a genus of greenish fruticose lichens that grow in the form of flattened, strap-like branches. Members of the genus are commonly called strap lichens or cartilage lichens. Apothecia are lecanorine.
It is in the family Ramalinaceae and in the suborder Lecanorineae.

## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in humid, clean environments with filtered light. Provide high ambient humidity through misting or a humid enclosure, and ensure good air circulation. Traditional watering and fertilization are unnecessary and can be detrimental to its growth.
