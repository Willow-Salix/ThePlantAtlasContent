## Introduction
Staurothele is a genus of saxicolous (rock-dwelling), crustose lichens in the family Verrucariaceae. It has about 40 species. When the fungus is part of a lichen, the genus of lichen is commonly called rock pimples.

## Care Guide
Wart Lichen thrives on calcareous rocks in temperate, moderate-light environments. Its care involves mimicking natural wet-dry cycles through periodic hydration and allowing full desiccation, with no need for fertilizers.
