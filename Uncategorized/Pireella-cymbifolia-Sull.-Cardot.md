## Care Guide
Pireella cymbifolia requires conditions mirroring its native humid tropical and subtropical forest environment. Ensure low to moderate indirect light, consistently high humidity, and frequent moisture to keep its substrate damp. Fertilization should be very minimal, as it primarily absorbs nutrients from its environment.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilization is required. If desired, a highly diluted (1/8 to 1/4 strength) liquid fertilizer formulated for epiphytes or delicate plants can be applied very sparingly (once or twice a year during active growth).
