## Introduction
Corylus americana, the American hazelnut or American hazel, is a species of deciduous shrub in the genus Corylus, native to the eastern and central United States and extreme southern parts of eastern and central Canada.

## Care Guide
The American Hazelnut, a native of temperate deciduous forests, thrives in full sun to partial shade with consistently moist, well-drained soil. It requires regular watering when young or during dry periods, but is relatively low maintenance once established, with minimal fertilization needs.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. Generally does not require heavy fertilization. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring can be beneficial in poor soils or for increased production.
