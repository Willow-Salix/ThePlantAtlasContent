## Care Guide
This temperate forest moss thrives in bright indirect light or partial shade with high humidity. Maintain consistent moisture by regular misting and watering, ensuring it never dries out completely but also isn't waterlogged. Fertilization is rarely, if ever, needed.

### Fertilizer
Very low; typically satisfied by trace elements in rainwater and its substrate. Excessive fertilization is detrimental.
