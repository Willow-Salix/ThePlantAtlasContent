## Introduction
Lilium philadelphicum, also known as the wood lily, flame lily, Philadelphia lily,  (wild) tiger lily, prairie lily, or western red lily, is a perennial species of lily native to North America.

## Care Guide
The Wood Lily thrives in temperate climates, preferring full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-draining soil. Provide regular watering during its growing season and a moderate amount of fertilizer, ensuring a drier period for dormancy.

### Fertilizer
Benefits from a balanced, slow-release granular fertilizer applied in early spring, or a diluted liquid fertilizer every 2-4 weeks during active growth, especially in nutrient-poor soils.
