## Care Guide
Garrett's Bristle Moss thrives in cool, humid conditions, similar to its native montane forest environment. Provide partial shade to dappled light and ensure high ambient humidity through regular misting. This moss has very low nutrient requirements, so fertilization is generally not needed.

### Fertilizer
Very low to none. Mosses typically derive sufficient nutrients from rainwater, atmospheric dust, and trace elements from their substrate. Over-fertilization can be detrimental.
