## Care Guide
To care for Shrubby Milkwort, emulate its native arid habitat by providing full to partial sun and ensuring excellent drainage. Water infrequently but deeply, allowing the soil to dry thoroughly between sessions, and keep fertilization minimal for optimal growth.

### Fertilizer
