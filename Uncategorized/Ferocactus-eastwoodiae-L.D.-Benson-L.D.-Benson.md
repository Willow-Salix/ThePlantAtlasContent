## Introduction
Ferocactus cylindraceus is a species of barrel cactus which is known by several common names, including California barrel cactus, Desert barrel cactus, compass barrel cactus, and miner's compass. It was first described by George Engelmann in 1853.

## Care Guide
As a desert plant, the California Barrel Cactus demands full sun exposure and extremely infrequent watering, allowing its soil to thoroughly dry out. Provide excellent drainage and minimal fertilization to replicate its arid native conditions and prevent root rot.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to none. If fertilizing, use a balanced, low-nitrogen cactus-specific fertilizer diluted to half strength, once or twice during the active growing season (spring/early summer).
