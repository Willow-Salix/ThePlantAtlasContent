## Care Guide
This Rocky Mountain variety of Lupine thrives in full sun and well-drained, lean soil. Water sparingly once established, allowing the soil to dry thoroughly between waterings. Avoid over-fertilizing as it is a nitrogen fixer.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilization. As a legume, it fixes nitrogen and thrives in lean to moderately fertile soils. Excessive fertilizer can be detrimental.
