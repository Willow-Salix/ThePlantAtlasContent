## Care Guide
This epiphytic lichen flourishes in conditions mirroring its native tropical and subtropical humid forests, requiring high ambient humidity and low to medium indirect light. Consistent moisture through regular misting is crucial, while traditional watering and fertilization are generally not needed for its survival.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Lichens absorb nutrients directly from the atmosphere and rainwater; traditional fertilizers are generally unnecessary and can be harmful.
