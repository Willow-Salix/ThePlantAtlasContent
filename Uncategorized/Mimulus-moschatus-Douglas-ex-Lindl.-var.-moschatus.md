## Introduction
Erythranthe moschata is a species of monkeyflower known by the common names muskflower, musk monkeyflower, and formerly as the common musk, eyebright and musk plant. It was formerly known as Mimulus moschatus.

## Care Guide
Muskflower thrives in environments that replicate its native temperate moist woodlands. Provide consistent moisture, ensuring the soil never dries out, and locate it in partial to full shade to protect it from intense sun. A moderate feeding schedule during its growth period will support its health.

### Fertilizer
Moderate. Benefits from a balanced liquid fertilizer diluted to half strength monthly during the active growing season.
