## Care Guide
Harvestbells prefer consistently moist, well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. Ensure regular watering, especially during dry spells, and apply a moderate, balanced fertilizer in spring to encourage robust growth and blooms.

### Fertilizer
Moderate; benefits from a balanced, slow-release fertilizer or a top-dressing of compost in spring to support healthy growth and flowering.
