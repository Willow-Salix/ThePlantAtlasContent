## Care Guide
This resilient Mediterranean native thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for xeriscaping or dry gardens. Water infrequently, allowing the soil to dry between sessions, and provide minimal fertilization, particularly avoiding excess nitrogen.

### Fertilizer
Low fertilizer needs. A light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in spring, or none at all, is usually sufficient. Avoid high nitrogen fertilizers as it's a legume.
