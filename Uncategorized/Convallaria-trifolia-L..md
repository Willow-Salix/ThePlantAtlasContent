## Introduction
Convallaria is a genus of flowering plants, commonly known as lily-of-the-valley. It includes three species native to temperate Eurasia and the east-central United States.

The generic name means valley in Botanical Latin, in reference to the plant's natural geographical habitat.

## Care Guide
This plant thrives in cool, consistently wet environments with ample shade, replicating its native bog and wet forest habitats. Ensure the soil never dries out and provide only minimal fertilization, as it is adapted to nutrient-poor conditions.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate. A dilute, balanced liquid fertilizer applied sparingly during the growing season is sufficient, as native bog soils are often nutrient-poor and acidic.
