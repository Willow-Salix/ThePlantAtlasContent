## Care Guide
Arizona Buckthorn thrives in full sun and well-drained, lean soils, typical of its arid and semi-arid native range. Once established, it requires infrequent, deep watering and is very drought-tolerant. Fertilization is rarely needed, as it is adapted to low-nutrient environments.

### Fertilizer
Low to no fertilizer required. Its native soils are typically rocky and nutrient-poor, so it is adapted to lean conditions. If desired, a very light application of a balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring can be given, but it is rarely necessary.
