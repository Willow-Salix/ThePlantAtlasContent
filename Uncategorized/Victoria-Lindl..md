## Introduction
Victoria or giant waterlily is a genus of aquatic herbs in the plant family Nymphaeaceae. The leaves are a remarkable size; Victoria boliviana produces leaves up to 3.2 metres (10 ft) in width. The genus name was given in honour of Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom.

## Care Guide
The Giant Waterlily thrives in full sun, requiring constant submersion in warm, fresh water within a large pond or aquatic container. It is a heavy feeder, necessitating regular fertilization in the substrate or water column to support its rapid growth. Mimicking its tropical river basin origins ensures vigorous development.
