## Care Guide
Whitewash cornflower is a resilient perennial that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, characteristic of its native montane grassland environment. It is drought-tolerant once established, requiring moderate watering and minimal fertilization to produce its vibrant blooms.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; thrives in average to poor, well-draining soils. Excessive fertilization is not necessary and may lead to leggy growth.
