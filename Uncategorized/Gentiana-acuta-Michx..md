## Introduction
Gentianella amarella, the autumn gentian, autumn dwarf gentian, or autumn felwort, is a short biennial plant  flowering plant in the gentian family, Gentianaceae. It is found throughout Northern Europe, the western and northern United States, and Canada.

## Care Guide
This Autumn Gentian thrives in cool, temperate to subalpine environments, favoring full sun to partial shade. It requires consistently moist, well-drained soil, mirroring its native habitat in wet meadows and open woodlands. Provide low to moderate fertilization to encourage robust growth.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; benefits from organic matter or a balanced, slow-release fertilizer applied sparingly.
