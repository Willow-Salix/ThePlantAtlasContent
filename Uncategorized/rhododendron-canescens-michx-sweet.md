To mimic its native southeastern US woodland habitat, provide Mountain Azaleas with dappled sunlight or morning sun and consistently moist, acidic, well-drained soil. Regular watering, especially in dry periods, and a light spring application of acid-specific fertilizer will promote vigorous growth and abundant flowering.
