Mourning Milkvetch thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, reflecting its arid montane native habitat. It is highly drought-tolerant, requiring infrequent watering, and has low fertilizer needs due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities. Avoid overwatering to ensure plant health.

### Fertilizer
