## ðŸŒ± Overview
Taro (; Colocasia esculenta) is a root vegetable. It is the most widely cultivated species of several plants in the family Araceae that are used as vegetables for their corms, leaves, stems and petioles. Taro corms are a food staple in African, Oceanian, East Asian, Southeast Asian and South Asian cultures (similar to yams).
