The Santa Cruz water-lily is a tropical aquatic plant that thrives in full sun and requires constant submersion in warm water. It is a heavy feeder, demanding regular fertilization to support its rapid growth and large, iconic leaves and flowers. Maintaining consistent water levels and warm temperatures is essential for its successful cultivation.
