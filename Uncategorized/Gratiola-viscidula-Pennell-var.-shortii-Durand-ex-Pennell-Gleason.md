## Introduction
Gratiola is a genus of plants in the family Plantaginaceae. Most species are known generally as hedgehyssops. It includes 29 species native to temperate North America and Eurasia, and to Morocco, South America, Australia, and New Zealand. The genus was previously included in the family Scrophulariaceae.

## Care Guide
This wetland plant thrives in consistently saturated or standing water and requires full sun exposure to mimic its native boggy environment. Provide a moderately fertile soil rich in organic matter to support healthy growth. Ensure the plant never dries out to maintain its health and vigor, especially during warmer months.

### Fertilizer
