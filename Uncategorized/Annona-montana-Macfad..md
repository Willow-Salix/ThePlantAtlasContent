## Introduction
Annona montana, the mountain soursop, is a tree and its edible fruit in the Annonaceae family native to Central America, the Amazon, and islands in the Caribbean. It has fibrous fruits. A. montana may be used as a rootstock for cultivated Annonas.

## Care Guide
Thrive in warm, humid conditions with consistently moist, well-drained soil, mimicking its tropical forest origin. Provide partial to full sun for optimal growth and fruit production, and fertilize regularly to support its vigorous fruiting cycle.

### Fertilizer
Benefits from regular feeding, especially a balanced fertilizer during the growing season and one higher in phosphorus and potassium when flowering and fruiting.
