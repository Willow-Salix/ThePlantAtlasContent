## Care Guide

It benefits from light to moderate fertilization in warmer months. Mimicking its tropical grassland origins, it prefers open, warm environments.

Light to moderate fertilization with a balanced, slow-release granular fertilizer during the active growing season is beneficial, especially in poorer soils.
