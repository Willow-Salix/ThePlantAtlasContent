## Introduction
Fraxinus ornus, the manna ash or South European flowering ash, is a species of Fraxinus native to Southern Europe and Southwestern Asia, from Spain and Italy north to Austria and the Czech Republic, and east through the Balkans, Turkey, and western Syria to Lebanon and Armenia.

## Care Guide
This tree thrives in full sun and is highly drought-tolerant once established, mirroring its Mediterranean origins. Provide moderate water during the growing season and fertilize sparingly, especially in well-drained, average soils, to maintain its health and vigor.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate fertilization needs, thriving in average to poor soils. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer in early spring can benefit young trees or those in very lean soils.
