## Care Guide
Water Chickweed thrives in consistently wet conditions, often growing in shallow water or boggy areas. It prefers partial to full sun and does not require heavy fertilization. Mimic its native wetland habitat for optimal growth.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; a balanced liquid fertilizer can be applied sparingly during the growing season, but it often thrives without heavy feeding in naturally rich organic soils.
