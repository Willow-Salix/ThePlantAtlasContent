## Introduction
Protoblastenia is a genus of lichens in the family Psoraceae. It was originally circumscribed by Alexander Zahlbruckner in 1908 as a section of genus Blastenia. J. Steiner promoted it to generic status in 1911.

## Care Guide
This saxicolous lichen thrives on exposed, acid rocks in full sun within temperate and boreal regions. It requires periodic rehydration from environmental moisture (rain, mist, dew) followed by periods of complete desiccation. Avoid fertilizers and maintain good air quality, as it is sensitive to pollutants.
