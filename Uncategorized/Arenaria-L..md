## Care Guide
Sandworts require well-drained soil, full to partial sun, and moderate watering, ensuring the soil dries between applications to prevent rot. They are adapted to lean soils, so minimal fertilization is needed.

### Fertilizer
Low fertilizer needs. Native to often lean, nutrient-poor soils, so a light application of balanced fertilizer once a year in spring, or none if soil is already reasonably fertile, is sufficient.
