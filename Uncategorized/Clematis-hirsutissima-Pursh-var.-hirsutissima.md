## Care Guide
Hairy Clematis thrives in full sun to partial shade, mirroring its native open montane woodland and grassland environments. It prefers moderate watering, tolerating dry spells once established, and requires minimal fertilizer as it is adapted to lean soils. Ensure good drainage for this resilient plant.
