## Introduction
Lonicera ciliosa, the orange honeysuckle or western trumpet honeysuckle is a honeysuckle native to forests of western North America.

## Care Guide
This deciduous vine thrives in its native temperate woodland climate with a balance of sun and consistent moisture. Provide a sturdy support for climbing and ensure well-draining soil. Minimal feeding is typically required, with a light annual application in spring.

### Fertilizer
Light to moderate feeding. A balanced, slow-release fertilizer applied in early spring is beneficial, especially in nutrient-poor soils.
