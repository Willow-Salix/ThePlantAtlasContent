Lupinus, commonly known as lupin, lupine, or regionally bluebonnet, is a genus of plants in the legume family Fabaceae. The genus includes over 199 species, with centres of diversity in North and South America. Smaller centres occur in North Africa and the Mediterranean. Saffron-flowered Lupine thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, mirroring its native Mediterranean habitat. Provide moderate water during its spring growth, ensuring soil dries out, and reduce significantly or withhold water during its summer dormancy. Due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities, avoid high-nitrogen fertilizers.
