## Introduction
Grimmia is a genus of mosses, originally named by Jakob Friedrich Ehrhart in honour of Johann Friedrich Carl Grimm, a physician and botanist from Gotha, Germany.

## Care Guide
Grimmia montana is a resilient moss adapted to harsh montane environments. Provide full to partial sun exposure and allow it to dry out extensively between infrequent waterings. Fertilization is generally not needed due to its low nutrient requirements.
