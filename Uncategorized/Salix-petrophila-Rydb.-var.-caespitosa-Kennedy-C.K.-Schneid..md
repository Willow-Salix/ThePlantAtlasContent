## Care Guide
This plant thrives in full sun with consistently moist yet exceptionally well-drained soil, mimicking its alpine habitat. It requires minimal fertilizer and is highly tolerant of cold temperatures, making good drainage paramount for successful cultivation.

### Fertilizer
Very low to no fertilizer needed; native alpine soils are typically lean and nutrient-poor, so excessive fertilization can be detrimental.
