## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in high humidity and indirect light, much like its native tropical forest habitat. Ensure consistent atmospheric moisture through misting or a humid environment, as it absorbs water directly from the air. Traditional soil watering and fertilization are unnecessary and should be avoided.
