## Care Guide
This moss thrives in environments mimicking its native humid tropical montane habitats. Provide consistent moisture and very high humidity, along with low to indirect bright light to prevent drying. Fertilization should be minimal to none.

### Fertilizer
Very minimal; mosses are low nutrient consumers. A highly diluted (1/4 strength) balanced liquid fertilizer can be applied once or twice a year during active growth, or often none is needed.
