## Introduction
Dieteria is a North American genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.
The genus Dieteria is closely related to Machaeranthera but distinguished  by having entire to toothed leaves, whereas Machaeranthera has once or twice pinnate leaves.

## Care Guide
This tough desert native requires full sun and extremely infrequent watering, tolerating drought conditions well. It prefers lean, well-draining soils and generally does not need fertilization to thrive, making it a low-maintenance plant for arid conditions.
