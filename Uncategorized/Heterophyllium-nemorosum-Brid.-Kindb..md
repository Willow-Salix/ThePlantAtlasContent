## Care Guide
This moss requires a consistently moist, but not waterlogged, substrate and high humidity. It thrives in deep shade or very low indirect light, making it suitable for terrariums or shaded garden spots. Fertilization is rarely necessary.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to none. Typically thrives on nutrients from decaying organic matter present in its substrate.
