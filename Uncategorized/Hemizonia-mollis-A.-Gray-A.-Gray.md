## Introduction

## Care Guide
Soft Tarweed is a California native adapted to full sun and dry Mediterranean summers. It requires infrequent watering, thriving in drought-like conditions once established, particularly during its summer dormancy. Minimal fertilization is needed, ideally during its cooler active growth period.
