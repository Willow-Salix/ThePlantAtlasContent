## Introduction
Candelariella is a genus of bright yellow, ocher, or greenish yellow crustose or squamulose lichens in the family Candelariaceae. Members of the genus are commonly called eggyolk lichens, goldspeck lichens, or yolk lichens.

## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in environments mimicking temperate deciduous forests. Provide moderate indirect light, high humidity, and regular moisture through misting. Avoid traditional watering and fertilization, as lichens are sensitive to excess nutrients and can be harmed by them.
