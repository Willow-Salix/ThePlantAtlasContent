## Care Guide
This moss requires a consistently humid, shaded environment to mimic its native coastal forest habitat. Ensure the substrate remains moist without being waterlogged, and protect it from any direct sunlight. Fertilization is generally not necessary, as it has very low nutrient demands.

### Fertilizer
Minimal to no fertilizer. Mosses derive most nutrients from water and the air; over-fertilization can be harmful. If growth seems poor, a very dilute, balanced liquid fertilizer (1/8 strength) can be applied very sparingly once a year.
