## Care Guide
Wild Basil thrives in temperate climates, preferring full sun to partial shade and consistently moist, well-drained soil. It is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and minimal fertilization, making it suitable for naturalized areas or herb gardens.

### Fertilizer
Low to moderate; a balanced slow-release fertilizer once in spring, or none if soil is naturally fertile.
