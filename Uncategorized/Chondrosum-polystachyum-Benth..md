## Care Guide
This resilient grass thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, reflecting its native arid environment. Water deeply but infrequently, allowing the soil to dry out completely between sessions, and avoid over-fertilization due to its adaptation to lean soil conditions. Ensure excellent drainage to prevent root issues.

### Fertilizer
