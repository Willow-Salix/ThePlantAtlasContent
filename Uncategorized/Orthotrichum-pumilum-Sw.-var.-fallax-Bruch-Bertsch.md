## Care Guide
This moss thrives in conditions mimicking its temperate forest habitat, requiring consistent high humidity and protection from direct sunlight. Ensure regular moisture by misting or providing a humid environment, avoiding prolonged waterlogging. Fertilization is generally unnecessary and can be detrimental to its health.

### Fertilizer
No specific fertilizer required. Mosses derive sufficient nutrients from rainwater and airborne particles; excess fertilization can be harmful.
