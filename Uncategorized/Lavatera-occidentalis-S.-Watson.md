## Care Guide
Island Mallow is a drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and lean, well-draining soils, mimicking its arid coastal scrubland origins. It requires infrequent watering and minimal fertilization once established, making it an excellent low-maintenance choice for xeriscaping.
