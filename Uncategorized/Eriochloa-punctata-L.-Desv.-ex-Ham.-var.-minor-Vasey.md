## Care Guide
This tropical and subtropical grass thrives in open, sunny locations with consistently moist soil, typical of its native wetland and grassland habitats. It requires moderate fertility and consistent watering to flourish, making it suitable for areas with ample light and moisture.

### Fertilizer
Moderate; benefits from a balanced, general-purpose fertilizer during its active growing season, particularly in nutrient-poor soils.
