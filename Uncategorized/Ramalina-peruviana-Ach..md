## Introduction
Ramalina is a genus of greenish fruticose lichens that grow in the form of flattened, strap-like branches. Members of the genus are commonly called strap lichens or cartilage lichens. Apothecia are lecanorine.
It is in the family Ramalinaceae and in the suborder Lecanorineae.

## Care Guide
This lichen thrives in bright light and very high atmospheric humidity, replicating its native fog desert environment. Ensure excellent air circulation to prevent prolonged wetness, and avoid any direct watering or fertilization as it obtains nutrients from the air.
