## Care Guide
Thriving in full sun and well-drained conditions, the Silver Dwarf Morning-Glory prefers its soil to dry slightly between waterings, mimicking its native subtropical open habitats. It is a light feeder, requiring minimal fertilization to promote abundant flowering.

### Fertilizer
Light feeder. A balanced, slow-release granular fertilizer applied once or twice during the active growing season, or a liquid fertilizer at half strength monthly, is usually sufficient.
