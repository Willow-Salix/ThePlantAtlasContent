## Care Guide
Warnstorfia Moss thrives in consistently wet to saturated environments, similar to its native fen and bog habitats. Provide bright, indirect light and maintain very high humidity, ensuring the substrate never dries out. It requires minimal to no fertilization due to its adaptation to nutrient-poor conditions.

### Fertilizer
Extremely low nutrient needs. Avoid traditional fertilizers; if used at all, apply a highly diluted, specialized moss or aquatic plant fertilizer sparingly once or twice a year.
