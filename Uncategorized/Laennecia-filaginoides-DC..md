## Introduction

## Care Guide
This desert native thrives in full sun and requires minimal watering, preferring to dry out between waterings. It is highly drought-tolerant and adapted to lean soils, meaning very little to no fertilization is needed.

### Fertilizer
Low fertilizer needs. If necessary, a very dilute, balanced fertilizer can be applied once in spring, but it generally thrives in lean, poor soils.
