## Care Guide
Philadelphia wildrye thrives in full sun to partial shade, mirroring its temperate meadow and riparian origins in Western North America. It requires consistently moist soil, benefiting from regular watering and low to moderate fertilization to support its growth as a native grass.

### Fertilizer
