Acacia cavenia, or Espino, thrives in full sun and is exceptionally drought-tolerant, requiring minimal watering once established. It prefers well-draining soil and does not generally need significant fertilization. Mimicking its native semi-arid and Mediterranean conditions is key to its successful growth.
