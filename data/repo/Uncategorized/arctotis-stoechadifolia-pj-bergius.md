African daisies thrive in full sun and well-draining soil, mimicking their native Mediterranean climate in South Africa. They are relatively drought-tolerant once established, preferring moderate watering and requiring only light fertilization to encourage their vibrant blooms. Ensure good air circulation and avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
