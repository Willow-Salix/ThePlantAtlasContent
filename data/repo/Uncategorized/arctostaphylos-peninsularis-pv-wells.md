Peninsular Manzanita thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, mimicking its native Mediterranean chaparral habitat. It is highly drought-tolerant once established and requires minimal to no supplemental water, especially during warm seasons, and very little fertilizer. Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
