The Mugo Pine thrives in full sun and well-draining, even rocky, soil, mirroring its native mountainous environment. It requires moderate watering initially, becoming highly drought-tolerant once established, and generally prefers lean soil conditions rather than heavy fertilization. Ensure excellent drainage to prevent root rot.
